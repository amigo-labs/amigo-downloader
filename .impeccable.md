# amigo-downloader Design System

## Design Context

### Users
Power users and tech enthusiasts managing downloads (HTTP, Usenet) in a centralized platform. They prefer dark mode, need instant orientation, and rely on status indicators (color, glow, badges). The app is used on both desktop and mobile.

### Brand Personality
**Technical, Precise, Powerful**

- Voice: Direct, competent, no unnecessary words
- Tone: Professional with a hint of cyberpunk aesthetics
- Emotional goal: Confident control AND visual pride — the tool looks so good you want to show it off

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Corporate Neon — professional at rest, electric on interaction
- **Theme**: Custom Neon Theme System, 6 color palettes (Blue, Teal, Indigo, Amber, Violet, Rose)
- **Dark Mode**: Primary mode, "Lights On" as alternative
- **Neon Intensity**: 5 levels (Off to Full), CRT scanlines and letter flicker only at Full
- **References**: No direct inspirations — amigo-downloader defines its own look as a hybrid of download manager and neon aesthetics
- **Anti-References**: Generic Bootstrap dashboards, bloated enterprise UIs, playful consumer apps

### Design Principles

1. **Glow Follows Function**: Neon effects reinforce information hierarchy — status dots glow by severity, active elements illuminate, inactive ones are dimmed.
2. **Instant Orientation**: Status, speed, and progress visible at a glance. No hidden information behind clicks.
3. **Adjustable Intensity**: The user decides how much cyberpunk they want — from "Off" (clean professional) to "Full" (CRT scanlines, flicker). No forced style.
4. **Responsive Without Compromise**: Mobile is not an afterthought — every page works at 320px just as well as at 2560px.
5. **Respect Accessibility**: `prefers-reduced-motion` disables all animations. Color contrasts remain WCAG AA compliant across all themes.

### Design Tokens

- **Typography**: Inter (Body/Headings), ui-monospace stack (Code/Labels), Major Third Scale (0.75rem - 2.5rem)
- **Breakpoint**: 768px (single primary breakpoint)
- **Spacing**: 0.25rem - 4rem scale, page-container 2rem/1rem (desktop/mobile)
- **Cards**: neon-border with hover glow, position relative for scanline overlay (Full intensity only)
- **Status Colors**: status-online/#22c55e (Complete), neon-warning/#eab308 (Pending), status-error/#ef4444 (Error)
- **Neon Glow Tokens**: --neon-glow-sm, --neon-glow-md, --neon-glow-lg, --neon-text-glow, --neon-border, --neon-border-hover (all scaled by intensity)
- **60:30:10 Rule**: 60% dark backgrounds (bg-deep, bg-surface), 30% secondary surfaces/text/borders, 10% neon accents (CTAs, active states, progress)
